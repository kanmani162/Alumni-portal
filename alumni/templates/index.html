About Bharathidasan University

Bharathidasan University established in February 1982 was named after the great revolutionary Tamil Poet, Bharathidasan (1891-1964). The motto of the University "We will create a brave new world" has been framed from Bharathidasan's poetic words "புதியதோர் உலகம் செய்வோம்". The University endeavours to be true to such a vision by creating in the region a brave new world of academic innovation for social change.

School of Computer Science, Engineering & Applications

About Department

The Department of Computer Engineering and Technology, founded in the academic year 2008–2009, is a vital part of the School of Computer Science, Engineering and Applications at Bharathidasan University, located in the Khajamalai City Campus. With a strong focus on academic excellence and research innovation, the department equips students with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills needed to thrive in today’s tech-driven world. Modern computing infrastructure, combined with experienced faculty, ensures that students receive a comprehensive education. Through internships, in-plant training, and technical workshops, learners are given ample exposure to real-world applications and industry practices. The department is known for nurturing innovative ideas and fostering research collaborations, including several externally funded projects.

To keep pace with evolving technologies, the curriculum is regularly reviewed and updated by a panel of academic and industry experts. The department also houses a well-stocked library with over 3,700 books and a digital collection of more than 100 e-books, supporting a rich academic environment. As an active member of IEEE and ACM, it offers access to leading journals and publications.

Research within the department spans various emerging domains such as Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Semantic Web, Ontology, Networks, QoS, and Bioinformatics.The presence of the Bharathidasan University Technology Park (BUTP), established in 2005, further strengthens the department’s commitment to bridging the gap between academia and industry, serving as a pioneering initiative in promoting IT development in Tiruchirappalli.
